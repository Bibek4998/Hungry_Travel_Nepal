Embark on an epic paragliding adventure in the enchanting city of Pokhara, Nepal, where every moment is a breathtaking journey into the skies.
Now jump!
When planning your paragliding escapade from Kathmandu to Pokhara, it's essential to pick a day with clear weather conditions for the best flying experience. Opt for a day when the skies are unobscured by clouds, typically during the dry season from October to April. Upon arrival in Pokhara, head straight to the paragliding launch site at Sarangkot, located on a hilltop overlooking the city and Phewa Lake. This iconic spot offers the perfect vantage point for taking off into the sky and experiencing the thrill of paragliding amidst breathtaking mountain scenery.
