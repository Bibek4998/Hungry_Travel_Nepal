WHITE WATER RAFTING !!!

Rafting on the Bhote Kosi is a full body experience, much more than simply paddling forward and backward...

White water rafting is also one of the adventure sports we include in teambuilding, especially in school expeditions where they embark on an exhilarating rafting expedition along the Bhote Koshi River. Guided by experienced rafting instructors, participants navigate through thrilling rapids, enjoying the stunning natural beauty and the thrill of working together as a team.Tumbling from the snow-capped peaks of Tibet, the Bhote Kosi river provides some of the most exciting rafting and kayaking in Nepal, and is quite possibly one of the best short river trips offered anywhere in the world.
